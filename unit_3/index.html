No limitations, no restrictions, no curbs—because nothing should stand between you and your rainbath.

Compromise nothing. At De-Curb, we believe in accessibility and we believe in good design. In fact, we think you can’t have one without the other. No matter your budget, needs, or tastes we’ll find a showering solution that works for you and makes you happy. After all, there’s nothing like a good shower, a morning or eveing bracer, to revive the senses and awakens the soul. We mean that.

Why go curbless?

  • Less Clutter
  • Keeps water in its place
  • Easier to clean
  • Makes your bathroom seem larger
  • Improves accessibility

Need more reasons?

A curbless shower is the perfect compromise (and a beautiful one!) when it comes to accommodating the needs of everyone in your home. It offers people of all ages the freedom to safely enter the shower without the need to climb over a threshold or into a tub. A curbless shower is the perfect solution that will cater to everyone’s needs and abilities.
